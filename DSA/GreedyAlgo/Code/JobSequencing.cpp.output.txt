=== Job Sequencing Problem (C++) ===

Jobs (ID, Deadline, Profit):
Job 1: Deadline 4, Profit 20
Job 2: Deadline 1, Profit 10
Job 3: Deadline 1, Profit 40
Job 4: Deadline 1, Profit 30
Job 5: Deadline 3, Profit 35

Selected Jobs (in execution order):
Slot 1: Job 3
Slot 3: Job 5
Slot 4: Job 1

Total Jobs: 3
Total Profit: 95

=== Greedy Strategy ===
1. Sort jobs by profit (descending)
2. For each job:
   - Find latest available slot before deadline
   - If slot found: schedule job there
3. Calculate total profit

=== Complexity ===
Time Complexity:  O(n²) worst case
Space Complexity: O(n)
Optimal: Yes (greedy optimal for this problem)
