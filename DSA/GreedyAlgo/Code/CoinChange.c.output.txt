=== Coin Change Problem (C) ===

Available Coins: 1 5 10 25 
Amount to Make: 41

Coin Change for amount 41:
Take coin of value 25
Take coin of value 10
Take coin of value 5
Take coin of value 1

Total Coins Used: 4
Amount Made: Complete

=== Greedy Strategy ===
1. Sort coins in descending order
2. For each coin (largest to smallest):
   - Use as many as possible
   - Subtract from amount
3. Continue until amount becomes 0

=== Complexity ===
Time Complexity:  O(n log n) for sorting + O(amount)
Space Complexity: O(1)
Note: Greedy works for standard coin systems
