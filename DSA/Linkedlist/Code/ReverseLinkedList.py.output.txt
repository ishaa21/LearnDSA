=== Reverse Linked List ===

Original list: 10 -> 20 -> 30 -> 40 -> 50 -> NULL
After iterative reverse: 50 -> 40 -> 30 -> 20 -> 10 -> NULL

Original list: 10 -> 20 -> 30 -> 40 -> 50 -> NULL
After recursive reverse: 50 -> 40 -> 30 -> 20 -> 10 -> NULL

Complexity Analysis:
Iterative reverse: O(n) time, O(1) space
Recursive reverse: O(n) time, O(n) space (call stack)
