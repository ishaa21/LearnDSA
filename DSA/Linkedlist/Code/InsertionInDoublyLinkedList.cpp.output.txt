=== Insertion in Doubly Linked List ===

After end insertions: 10 <-> 20 <-> 30 <-> NULL
After beginning insertion: 5 <-> 10 <-> 20 <-> 30 <-> NULL
After position insertion: 5 <-> 15 <-> 10 <-> 20 <-> 30 <-> NULL

Complexity Analysis:
Insert at beginning: O(1)
Insert at end: O(n)
Insert at position: O(n)
Space: O(1) for operation
