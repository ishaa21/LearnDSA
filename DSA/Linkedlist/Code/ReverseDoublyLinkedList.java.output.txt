=== Reverse Doubly Linked List ===

Original (Forward): 10 <-> 20 <-> 30 <-> 40 <-> 50 <-> NULL
Original (Backward): 50 <-> 40 <-> 30 <-> 20 <-> 10 <-> NULL

After reverse (Forward): 
50 <-> 40 <-> 30 <-> 20 <-> 10 <-> NULL
After reverse (Backward): 10 <-> 20 <-> 30 <-> 40 <-> 50 <-> NULL

Complexity Analysis:
Reverse: O(n) time, O(1) space
